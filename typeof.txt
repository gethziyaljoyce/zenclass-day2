typeof(1)

	It gives the output as Number.. the datatype of 1 is number.

typeof(1.1)

	It also returns the output as number..the datatype of 1.1 is number.

typeof('1.1')

	The datatype of '1.1' is string.

typeof(true)

	The datatype of true is boolean.

typeof(null)

	The datatype of null is object.

typeof(undefined)

	It returns undefined.

typeof([])

	Array is an object.

typeof({})

	It is an object.

typeof(NaN)

	The datatypeof NaN is number.
